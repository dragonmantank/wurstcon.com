WurstCon 2014

Slated for September 20, this aims to be the wurst conference of 2014. We’re gathering the best and brightest of the dimwitted PHP community together under no roof to wait in line for several hours on a brisk autumn Saturday in Chicago with the hope of sampling some of the finest grilled, tubular meat products available within the continental United States.

Come October 4, Hot Doug’s will be on permanent vacation, so the clock is ticking. Most of us also have real conferences to attend later in the year, so this is really the ideal time for such an excursion.

Interested? We'd love to have you! Feel free to RSVP via Facebook or watch #WurstCon to follow the conversation on Twitter.
